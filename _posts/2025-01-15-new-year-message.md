Dear friends and family of the Chorale Saint Padre Pio,

As we step into this new year, we do so with hearts full of gratitude. Looking back, 2024 was a year of growth, beautiful music, and deep community. We sang at countless Masses, organized a major concert, and welcomed new members into our family.

This year, we are excited to expand our repertoire and participate in the diocesan choir festival. We also hope to record some of our pieces to share with the wider community online.

We invite you to pray for us, come support us at Mass, and — if you feel the call — **join us**.

*"Sing to the Lord a new song; sing to the Lord, all the earth." — Psalm 96:1*
