This year's Easter concert was truly a moment of grace for all of us at the Chorale Saint Padre Pio. The church was full, the voices soared, and the Spirit was present.

We performed a beautiful programme including the **Exsultet**, a medley of Easter hymns in Lingala and French, and closed with a joyful **Alleluia** that had the entire congregation joining in.

A heartfelt thank you to our choir director for the months of preparation, to all the choir members who gave their time and dedication, and to the Paroisse de Kansanga for their continued support.

We look forward to our next major celebration — **Pentecost Sunday**. Rehearsals begin next Wednesday!
